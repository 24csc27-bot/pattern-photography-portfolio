About PixelPattern

PixelPattern is a smartphone photography project dedicated to discovering patterns in architecture, nature, and everyday environments. Through symmetry, textures and repetition, the project highlights how simple structures can create visually striking compositions. All photographs are captured using a smartphone camera, showing that creativity matters more than equipment.
